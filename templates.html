💡 Strategy Tips — How to Get Clients Fast

📍 Where to Find Businesses to DM
INSTAGRAM: → Search your city + "salon", "restaurant", "fashion", "events", "church" → Look at businesses with few followers or no professional flyer → DM them with the WhatsApp template FACEBOOK: → Join local buy/sell groups (e.g. "Buy & Sell Abuja", "Lagos Marketplace") → Join church/business groups in your area → Post the Facebook templates above PHYSICALLY: → Walk into local salons, restaurants, print shops → Show them your portfolio on your phone → Offer to design one for free or very cheap as a sample WHATSAPP: → Send the broadcast message to ALL your contacts → Ask friends to forward it to their contacts → Post the Instagram captions as your WhatsApp Status daily
⏰ Daily Outreach Routine (30 mins/day)
MORNING (15 mins): → Post 1 design on Instagram with a caption from the templates → Share the same post to your WhatsApp Status AFTERNOON (10 mins): → Find 5 businesses on Instagram that have no good flyer → Send them the WhatsApp/DM message (personalised!) EVENING (5 mins): → Follow up on anyone who opened but didn't reply → Check your Lead Tracker and update statuses DO THIS EVERY DAY. Even 2 clients per week = ₦7,000–₦20,000/week.
🧠 Things That Get More Replies
1. PERSONALISE every message. Use their real name and business name. 2. MENTION past clients — "I worked with Vickie Taste and FD Beauty Luxe" builds instant trust. 3. KEEP IT SHORT. Nobody reads long first messages. Get to the point fast. 4. SHOW SAMPLES immediately. Send your best 2-3 designs right in the first message. 5. ASK A QUESTION at the end — "Want to see some samples?" gets a reply. Statements don't. 6. FOLLOW UP once after 2-3 days. Most sales happen on the follow-up. 7. OFFER A FREE SAMPLE for new clients who are hesitant — one free design = long-term client.
